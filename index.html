My homeland Ghana

Ghana is a country located in West Africa, along the Gulf of Guinea and the Atlantic Ocean. Ghana has diverse ethnic groups, beautiful landscapes, and is famous for landmarks like Cape Coast Castle, Lake Volta, and Kakum National Park. It is also known for gold, cocoa production, and vibrant traditions like kente cloth, music, and festivals.
